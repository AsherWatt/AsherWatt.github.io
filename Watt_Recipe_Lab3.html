Pizza!

The easiest Pizza ever!

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 Cups flour
  • 1 Teaspoon Salt>
  • 1 Teaspoon Sugar>
  • 1 Teaspoon Yeast
  • 1 Cup Water
  • 1 Tablespoon oil
  • 1/4 Cup tomato sauce
  • 1 Teaspoon Italian Seasoning
  • 1 Teaspoon Garlic Powder
  • salt and pepper
  • Pepperoni
  • 5 Cups Cheese

Directions

  1. Mix the first 4 ingredients in a bowl
  2. Mix in the water and oil, add flour
  3. Place onto floured surface and kneed for about 2 minutes
  4. Place in greased bowl, make sure top is greased
  5. Cover and let rise for 20 minutes
  6. Place on greased 12in pizza pan
  7. Make into circle
  8. Place toppings onto top of pizza, tomato sauce first
  9. Add the meat
  10. Add the Cheese
  11. More meat
  12. More cheese
  13. Bake at 400 for 20 minutes or until crust is light brown
